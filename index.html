About ISTrain

Our Mission

The First Portuguese
Railway Engineering Team

ISTrain is a railway engineering student team from Instituto Superior Tecnico. Founded in 2024, we are the first Portuguese student team competing in the IMechE Railway Challenge.

Our mission is to design, build, test, and compete with an innovative reduced-scale locomotive, advancing railway technology through engineering and multidisciplinary collaboration.

History

How It All Began

ISTrain was born from the initiative of a small group of students encouraged by Professor Hugo Magalhaes. With no existing railway-focused student group in Portugal, ISTrain began from scratch, driven by enthusiasm and the desire to represent the country on an international stage.

Since its founding in December 2024, the team has united more than 30 students from multiple backgrounds with one shared goal: Andorinha.

The Name

Andorinha

When the Portuguese railway network began taking shape in the mid-19th century, Andorinha became a symbol of speed and national pride. Named after the swift swallow, it reflected the spirit of innovation of Portugal's early railway engineers.

Today, ISTrain carries that spirit forward. A reduced-scale electric locomotive built entirely by students, from concept sketch to competition platform.

About the Challenge

What is IMechE?

The IMechE Railway Challenge is one of the most demanding student engineering competitions in the railway sector.

Teams design, manufacture and demonstrate a 10¼-inch gauge reduced-scale locomotive, evaluated by an expert jury across multiple categories.

A National First

ISTrain is the first Portuguese university team ever to enter the IMechE Railway Challenge.
